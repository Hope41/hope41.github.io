Snakes And Ladders
Help Drillo get through 12 levels on his mission to destroy the X-Ray Orb. Collect upgrades for new abilities but be careful of the enemy side effects!

Use the arrow keys, WASD or ZQSD to move. Further controls are explained during gameplay.
This six-month project was a huge learning curve. I've written some articles for parts of it, but if you'd like me to do a write-up for a certain aspect, please email me!
