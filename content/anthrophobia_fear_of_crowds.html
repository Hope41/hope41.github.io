This experiment began with a dweet I made on the very last day of 2021. I felt it needed growing and began making a bigger version (this).

Making procedural creatures was surprisingly simple. The idea is to generate a simple box like creature, apply randomness to it's colour, body height, arm length, leg length, eye distance ect.., and then move each 'man' in a random direction, stop at intervals, blink and move it's eyes. Admittedly, the code is not as neat as I thought it was when I made it, but it works and is still useful.

As you probably guessed, the home page of this website is a greyscale version of this experiment.
