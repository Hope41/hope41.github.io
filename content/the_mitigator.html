The Mitigator

PLAY

Control the robot around 10 levels, collecting litter and using plants to overwhelm the enemy robots.

Use the arrows, WASD, or ZQSD to move. Press X or SPACE to use your upgrade. You can only activate upgrades with power, and can get more power by destroying enemies or collecting litter. Hold the left or right keys along with the up key to climb walls.

This project was developed from February to March for the Technocamps Game of Codes competition 2022 under the theme Combatting Climate Change.
