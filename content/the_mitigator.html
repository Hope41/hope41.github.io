The Mitigator

things/the_mitigator.html

Control the robot around 9 levels, collecting litter and using the power of plants to overwhelm enemy robots.

Arrows/WASD/ZQSD to move.
Press X or SPACE to use your upgrade.
You can only activate upgrades with power.
You can get more power by destroying enemies or collecting litter.
Hold the LEFT/RIGHT key and UP to climb walls.

This project was developed from February to March for the Technocamps Game of Codes 2022 competition under the theme Combatting Climate Change.
