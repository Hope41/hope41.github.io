The Mitigator

6 Mar. 2022
PLAY

Destroy Robots, Restore the Climate

Check out The Mitigator, my first successful JavaScript game, shortlisted for Technocamps' Game of Codes 2022!

In The Mitigator, you are thrown into an intense battle against an onslaught of Evil Robots. But this isn't your typical battle. Armed with plants, seed-bombs, and a weird duplication upgrade, you'll fight back while restoring the climate, turning destruction into growth. Can you outlast the invasion and bring nature back to its former glory?

Star on GitHub

How to Play

Jump with the arrow key, W, or Z. Use the left and right arrow keys, AD, or QD to move around. Jump when next to a wall to climb it. Press X or SPACE to use your upgrade.

Fast and Furious

The hordes of evil robots grow with every level. Make the most of your power-ups, since losing five lives will take you right back to the start of the game.

Free and Open Source!

All my games so far are free and have had their source code disclosed. Feel free to make your own versions of the game on GitHub! Licensed under CC-BY-SA-4.0.
