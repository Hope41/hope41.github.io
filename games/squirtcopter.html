Squirtcopter

28 Apr. 2024
PLAY

The Online Helicopter Game

Starting out as a courageous copter, use your power to squirt robots with particles, strategically upgrade your machine and squirt your way to victory in a battle to save the city!

An entry to the Gamedev.js Jam 2024, ranking in the top 17%.

Star on GitHub Watch the Trailer View on itch.io

How to Play

Fly upwards using the up arrow key, W, or Z. Use the left and right arrow keys, AD, or QD to move the copter through the world.

The copter will fire automatically through gameplay. You can upgrade the firing efficiency by landing on the power stations scattered across the world.

The Squirtcopter about to land on a power station

The aim of the game is to squirt robots. By avoiding the robots the game will not progress.

For the Odd Half Hour...

The average runtime for Squirtcopter is about 25 minutes, and is the sort of game that can be played over and over again: your Squirtcopter has fifteen potential upgrades to be unlocked, as well as a near infinite variety of enemies. My personal record stands at 18 minutes - let me know if you beat me to get a shout-out below!
